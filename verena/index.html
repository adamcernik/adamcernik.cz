About Me

Hello! I'm Verena, a UX Designer based in Vienna, Austria with 6+ years of experience crafting digital products that users love.

My design philosophy centers around user empathy, accessibility, and creating experiences that feel intuitive and delightful.

With a background in psychology and human-computer interaction, I approach design challenges with both analytical rigor and creative imagination.

My Process

01

Research

Understanding user needs, business requirements, and market context through interviews, surveys, and competitive analysis.

02

Define

Synthesizing research insights to create user personas, journey maps, and define clear problem statements.

03

Ideate

Generating solution concepts through sketching, wireframing, and collaborative design workshops.

04

Prototype

Building interactive prototypes that transform ideas into tangible experiences for testing and validation.

05

Test

Conducting usability testing to validate designs and gather feedback for iterations.

06

Implement

Collaborating with development teams to ensure design vision is implemented with attention to detail.
