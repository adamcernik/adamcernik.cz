Key Insights

  1. Device differences matter: Android users experienced more issues (especially with photo functionality) than iPhone users
  2. Role differences: Nurses seem to adapt more quickly to the recording interface than doctors
  3. Common pain points: Report generation wait time and ending visit flow are consistent issues across all users
  4. Different expectations: Users have varying expectations of AI capabilities - from simple transcription to advanced summarization
  5. Workflow integration: Users naturally integrate the app into their existing workflows (e.g., pausing to take photos of documents)
  6. Positive surprises: Auto-description features for photos and audio received enthusiastic responses
