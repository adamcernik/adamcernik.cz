Who We Are

WANTED s.f.o. was established in Prague, where we maintain our headquarters and production facilities. Since our founding, we have experienced significant growth in the quality and scope of our services, establishing ourselves firmly in the film industry, fit-out projects, custom furniture manufacturing, comprehensive interior design implementation, and sales support products.

Our Competitive Edge

Our distinct competitive advantage lies in our ability to execute projects through all divisions of our company. From project development, visualizations, and construction work to the production of custom interior elements and the supply of freestanding furniture—all under unified project management.
