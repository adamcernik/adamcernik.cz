I’m a UX & Product Designer with over 15 years of experience in crafting digital experiences that blend usability, aesthetics, and business impact. Most recently, I’ve been working independently, helping businesses refine their digital products through strategic UX and design consulting. Before that, I led design at Deutsche Bank, shaping the mobile banking experience and contributing to one of the first Apple Pay integrations in Europe. I also played a key role at Austrian Railways (ÖBB) and ORF, designing digital platforms used by millions. Prior to that, I worked with various startups and enterprises, focusing on interaction design, user research, and prototyping, ensuring products are not just functional but truly intuitive. Now, I’m working with select clients across Germany, Austria, and beyond, offering UX strategy, product design, and digital transformation expertise.

This page reflects my design philosophy—I create only what’s needed and communicate only what truly matters.
